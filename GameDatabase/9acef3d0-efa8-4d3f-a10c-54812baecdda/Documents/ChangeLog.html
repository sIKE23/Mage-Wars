1.14.0.0
    The Game:
    • A big thanks goes to Arcane Wonders for the following announcement! They are the ones that made this happen!
    • Academy Cards are now included and some are even automated! The cards currently are included for Arena matches. The module will be enhanced to include the Academy game in the future./li>

    • Fixed bug in Harshforge Monolith's Upkeep processing

    • Malakai's Fire no longer prompts when attacking a Conjuration
    • A creature with the Counterstrike trait should not start a Counterstrike when the originating attack was a Counterstrike
    • The Battle Calculator will now take the Spiked Bucklers Defense in account
    • You will now get the discount from Enchanters Ring when the target of the Enchantment is a Mage
    • Temple of Lights Attack has been automated
    • If you cancel a timer from clicking the X in the right hand corner of the modal window, you can now set another one later
    • Bloodthirsty now buffs attacks against damaged creatures with the plant subtype
    • Forcefield automation not working properly - Galvitar's Doublestrike attack will now target opposing Mage for the second attack if Reverse Attack is revealed
    • Conceding in a 3+ Free for all no longer ends the game
    • Many card fixes:
      • Fixed Ludwig Boltstorm's Triplestrike
      • Wispwillow Amulet is automated
      • Force Ring's discount is now taken into consideration if you are 1 mana short for casting the Spell
      • Blur is now automated, when you take damage you will be prompted to pay to keep it
      • Reinforces school is now correct
      • Venomous Zombie is now a full action to cast
      • Fixed Life Link's target
      • fixed set data for Messenger of Bim-Shalla

    • Updated MW-Cards.o8c, which contains all of the art needed to play Mage Wars including the new art images for Acadmey supplied by Arcane Wonders
